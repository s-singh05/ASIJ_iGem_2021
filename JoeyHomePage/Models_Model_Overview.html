Model Overview



Modeling serves as a powerful tool in Synthetic Biology, as it serves as a way to verify our findings and depict relationships between several parameters. In particular, since we were unable to conduct experiments in the fall, our models served as a way to strengthen our project and visualize our predicted results.

We first used mathematical modeling to derive the rate of interaction between the aptamer and biomarker to show the activity between them.

We also used protein modeling to model the interaction between aptamer and biomarker and visualize their interaction in a three dimensional manner.
